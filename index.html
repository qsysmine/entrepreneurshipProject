Introducing Rollteq

What is Rollteq?

Rollteq is the answer to an age-old question among runners: “How do you stretch while using ice or a heat pad?” Our answer is that you don't have to!

Rollteq is a foam roller that regulates its own temperature. Using a thermo-electric module, it heats itself up and cools itself down more quickly than putting a roller in the microwave and the fridge.


How do I use it?

Simply press the switch to the on position, move the temperature slider to warm or cold, and wait just 5 minutes and your roller should be ice-cold or fire-hot enough to help with your recovery or physical therapy!
